---
title: Upgrade to Pro
---

Follow the steps below to upgrade your existing `MasterInventoryEngine (Lite)` project to `MasterInventoryEngine Pro`.

1. Open your project that is using the `MasterInventoryEngine (Lite)` package.
Select the `MasterInventoryEngine` prefab in the scene.

---

2. In the **Inspector**, click **`Export Settings`** to Pro Version.
Save the exported settings as a .txt file.

---

3. **Completely remove** the entire `MasterInventoryEngine (Lite)` package from your project.

---

4. Download and import the `MasterInventoryEngine Pro` package.

---

5. Follow the [Installation](installation) page to complete the initial setup of the `Pro` version.

---

6. Navigate to:
`Project Settings → SoftKitty → SubData → Items`

---

7. Click `Import Settings From Lite Version`, then select the **.txt** file you exported in **Step 2**.

---

8. All inventory settings are now upgraded and ready to use in the `Pro` version.

---

9. Since `MasterInventoryEngine Pro` uses the [EntityManagerObject] to manage inventories, existing inventory data must be migrated from the old **`InventoryData`** component.

---

10. Select any GameObject that has an **`InventoryData`** component:

   - Enter a **UID** for this [Entity]

   - Click **`Transfer Data to Entity Manager`**

   - The inventory data will be copied into the [EntityManagerObject], and the [InventoryData] component will be automatically replaced by an [EntityComponent], which represents this [Entity] in the scene.


---
